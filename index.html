The Great British Bake Off

It's only right to first pay homage to past winners of the Great British Bake Off. The images below "Life After the Win" information where obtain from the following webpages The Great British Back Off page and the Standard Past Winners page

Edward

Season 1 Winner

Life after the win: Left debt collection to become a full-time food writer; authored three cookbooks and hosts the podcast Stir the Pot.

Edd Kimber Winner Season 1 (2010)

Joanne

Season 2 Winner

Life after the win: Released two bestselling cookbooks; runs cookery classes and has appeared on TV shows.

Joanne Wheatley Winner Season 2 (2011)

John

Season 3 Winner

Life after the win: Authored four cookbooks; runner-up on Strictly Come Dancing in 2021.

John Whaite Winner Season 3 (2012)

Frances

Season 4 Winner

Life after the win: Released a cookbook; baked for celebrities and appeared in Vogue.

Frances Quinn Winner Season 4 (2013)

Nancy

Season 5 Winner

Life after the win: Released a book; writes for The Daily Telegraph and campaigns for Love Food Hate Waste.

Nancy Birtwhistle Winner Season 5 (2014)

Nadiya

Season 6 Winner

Life after the win: Authored cookbooks; hosted TV shows and baked a cake for Queen Elizabeth's 90th birthday.

Nadiya Hussain Winner Season 6 (2015)

Candice

Season 7 Winner

Life after the win: Authored a cookbook; appeared on Loose Women and owns a pub.

Candice Brown Winner Season 7 (2016)

Sophie

Season 8 Winner

Life after the win: Became a track cyclist and rower; was training to be a stuntwoman and owns a cake business.

Sophie Faldo Winner Season 8 (2017)

Rahul

Season 9 Winner

Life after the win: Returned to work as an engineering researcher; however, occassionally appears on ITV's This Morning as a guest baker and wrote a food column for The Times.

Rahul Mandal Winner Season 9 (2018)

David

Season 10 Winner

Life after the win: Authored a children's cookbook; and takes part in the Sticky Boys Bun podcast.

David Atherton Winner Season 10 (2019)

Peter

Season 11 Winner

Life after the win: Completed his degree; authored a cookbook and offers online baking masterclasses.

Peter Sawkins Winner Season 11 (2020)

Giuseppe

Season 12 Winner

Life after the win: Authored two cookbooks; offers cooking classes.

Giuseppe Dell'Anno Winner Season 12 (2021)

Syabira

Season 13 Winner

Life after the win: Launched baking collections; hosts Malaysian cooking masterclasses and has a strong social media presence.

Dr Syabira Yusoff Winner Season 13 (2022)

Matty

Season 14 Winner

Life after the win: Left teaching to pursue baking; plans to write a book and start a cake business.

Matty Edgell Winner Season 14 (2023)

Georgie

Season 15 Winner

Life after the win: Still early days. Won the 2024 series; known for her summer garden celebration hanging cake.

Georgie Grasso Winner Season 15 (2024)
